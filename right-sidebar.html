Scholarship programs

Amerlia Earhart Fellowship

Zonta annually awards up to 30 Fellowships of US$10,000 each to women pursuing Ph.D./doctoral degrees in aerospace engineering and space sciences.

  • Learn More

Zonta Young Women in Leadership

Zonta recognizes young women for demonstrating leadership skills and commitment to public service with up to 37 awards of US$5,000 each annually.

  • Learn More

Zonta Women in STEM

Zonta encourages women to pursue education in STEM through a pilot program, offering up to 16 awards of US$10,000 and a complimentary one-year supporting membership in Zonta.

  • Learn More

Zonta Women in Buisness

Zonta celebrates women who demonstrate exceptional leadership and entrepreneurial spirit, offering 10 international awards of US$10,000 each and a complimentary one-year supporting membership in Zonta International for the next financial year.

  • Learn More
