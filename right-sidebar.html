Scholarships

Empowering Education and Leadership

The Zonta Club of Naples is deeply committed to advancing education, equality, and leadership opportunities for women and girls in our community and around the world. We believe that education is the foundation for empowerment and social change. Through local scholarships, mentorship programs, and youth engagement, Zonta Naples helps women reach their full potential and encourages them to take on leadership roles in their chosen fields. Our club also supports student-led Z and Golden Z Clubs in local schools, where young members learn the importance of service, advocacy, and personal development through hands-on community projects.

Scholarships and Programs

Each year, Zonta Naples awards scholarships to deserving women pursuing higher education, with a focus on those who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership promise, and a commitment to making a difference. Locally, we fund initiatives and educational grants that assist students with tuition and career advancement. On a global scale, our members proudly promote Zonta International’s educational programs- including the Amelia Earhart Fellowship for women in aerospace engineering, the Women in STEM and Business Scholarships, and the Young Women in Public Affairs Award. These programs open doors for women to break barriers in traditionally male-dominated fields and inspire the next generation of leaders.

Inspiring a Brighter Future

Our mission extends beyond financial assistance — it’s about fostering confidence, leadership, and long-term success. By investing in education and mentorship, Zonta Naples helps create pathways for women to lead with purpose and contribute meaningfully to their communities. We envision a future where every woman has the opportunity to learn, grow, and shape the world around her without limitations. Each scholarship awarded represents not just financial support, but a shared belief in potential, equality, and progress. Together, we are building a more inclusive and empowered future — one student, one leader, one dream at a time.

Memorial Scholarship Endowment Fund

On November 7, 2019 a Memorial Scholarship Endowed Fund was established at FGCU in the names of two past Zontians, Helen “Honey” Koenig Gardiner and Sally Sitta and The Zonta Club of Naples in the amount of $25,000.

Scholarship programs

Amelia Earhart Fellowship

Zonta annually awards up to 30 Fellowships of US$10,000 each to women pursuing Ph.D./doctoral degrees in aerospace engineering and space sciences.

Zonta Young Women in Leadership

Zonta recognizes young women for demonstrating leadership skills and commitment to public service with up to 37 awards of US$5,000 each annually.

  • Learn More

Zonta Women in STEM

Zonta encourages women to pursue education in STEM, offering up to 16 awards of US$10,000 & a complimentary one-year supporting membership in Zonta.

  • Learn More

Zonta Women in Buisness

Zonta celebrates women who demonstrate exceptional leadership and entrepreneurial spirit, offering 10 international awards of US$10,000 each & a free one-year supporting membership in Zonta International for the next financial year.
