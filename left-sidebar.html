Founded in 1919

Who We Are

The Zonta Club of Naples is part of a global network of professionals dedicated to advancing the status of women through service and advocacy. Founded on the principles of equality, respect, and collaboration, our club brings together members from diverse backgrounds who share a common goal — to empower women and make a lasting impact in our community. As one of over 1,100 clubs worldwide under Zonta International, we contribute to both local and global initiatives that support education, leadership, and gender equality. Zonta International, along with its districts and clubs, advocates at the international, national, and local levels on issues related to human rights and gender equality. At the international level, Zonta International works primarily through the United Nations and the Council of Europe to influence policies and programs that benefit women and girls. At the national level, Zonta districts and clubs take action to support or oppose legislation that affects women’s lives. Locally, clubs like Zonta Naples engage directly with community leaders, monitor policies, host public forums, and raise awareness about issues impacting women right here at home.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to build a better world for women and girls. Through service projects, advocacy, and educational initiatives, we work to improve lives and create opportunities for women to thrive. The Zonta Club of Naples focuses its projects on key issues affecting women and girls in Collier County, guided by the leadership of our Service Committee. This committee meets monthly to identify community needs, develop meaningful projects, and present recommendations to the board for approval. Once approved, all members are encouraged to participate in these efforts, ensuring that our mission is fulfilled through teamwork and shared purpose.

Making a Difference Together

At Zonta Naples, we believe that real change begins with connection — within our club, our community, and beyond. Our members unite their talents and passions to advocate for women’s rights, foster leadership, and build partnerships that strengthen our impact. Whether it’s organizing local outreach events, participating in public hearings, supporting scholarships, or joining international campaigns to end gender-based violence, we take pride in turning compassion into action. Together, we are part of a global movement that empowers women, uplifts communities, and builds a brighter, more equitable future for all.
